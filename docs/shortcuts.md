# ⌨️ Essential Keyboard Navigation

MemVault eliminates the need for a mouse when moving through your digital memory. Master these shortcuts to interact with your data instantly.

---

## 🧭 Zero-Click View Switching

MemVault organizes your entries into "Views." You can instantaneously snap between them using the primary number row on your keyboard. No loading or layout shifting.

- `1`: **All Entries (Timeline)** — The chronological master feed of everything (Diary, Convos, Worklogs, Docs, and Browser Output).
- `2`: **Diary Only** — Filters the view instantly to your personal and manual log entries.
- `3`: **Conversations Filter** — Isolates your Antigravity AI and ChatGPT conversation logs.
- `4`: **Worklogs Filter** — Shows only technical agent outputs, documentation, and specific Github commit histories.
- `5`: **Documentation Explorer** — Swaps out the timeline interface completely to render the internal markdown technical manuals (like this one you are reading).
- `6`: **Projects Dashboard** — Accesses the hub to create, navigate, and view AI-summaries of specific distinct projects.
- `V`: **Secure Vault** — Opens the AES-256-GCM encrypted panel for your secrets, passwords, and sensitive keys.
- `C`: **Calendar Review** — Unfolds the contribution heatmap for visual data exploration.

## ⚡ Productivity Actions

Never leave the keyboard when you need to capture a thought or find a specific string.

- `Alt + N`: **The Quick Entry Focus**. If you have an idea, hit this. It automatically routes your cursor to the primary "What's on your mind today?" Diary field so you can type and hit `Ctrl + Enter` immediately.
- `Ctrl + /` (or `Cmd + /`): **Global Fuzzy Search Focus**. This brings the cursor straight to the FTS5 search. Type `hydration bug` and hit `Enter` to find precisely when and how that problem was solved.
- `Esc`: **The Global Back Button**. Escape resets specific search filters, drops you back into the main timeline (`1`) from the Documentation Explorer, or clears active focus rings.
- `?`: **Shortcut Modal Toggle**. Unsure where you are? This flashes the floating shortcut reference guide directly on the screen.

---

## 🎨 Theme Command

- `T`: **Theme Toggle Mode**. Effortlessly swap between the `Aura` (Dark) theme and the `Clarity` (Light) theme based on the ambient context. Your preference is saved locally natively in the browser instance.
